## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons of olive oild
- 2 medium-sized onions
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 400 grams leek (2 medium-sized)
- 2 vegetable bouillon cubes
- 1 liter of water
- 15 grams of fresh mint
- 150 ml yoghurt
