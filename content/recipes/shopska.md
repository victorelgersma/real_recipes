## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- feta
- tomatoes
- olive oil
- salt, pepper
- (optional): roast peppers
- (optional): red peppers
- (optional): chickpeas
- (optional): Black olives

dressing: olive oil, lemon, vinegar, salt, pepper.
